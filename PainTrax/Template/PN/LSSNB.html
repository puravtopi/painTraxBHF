PREOPERATIVE DIAGNOSIS : Myalgia and myositis, bursitis, frozen left shoulder.

POSTOPERATIVE DIAGNOSIS : Myalgia and myositis, bursitis, frozen left shoulder.

PROCEDURE: Left shoulder suprascapular nerve block.

CONSENT: After the explanation of the risks, benefits, alternatives and possible complications, written and verbal informed consent was obtained from the patient and placed in the chart.

DESCRIPTION OF PROCEDURE: The patient placed in prone position. Left shoulder local under fluoroscopy 22-gauge needles were advanced to the suprascapular notch, localized with fluoroscopy and 5 cc of 0.5% Marcaine and 0.5% lidocaine injected into the area of block to release the shoulder joint. Sterile dressing applied at the injection site. The patient tolerated the procedure well.

Post op patient demonstrated a significant range of motion improvement. This will be considered as a positive suprascapular nerve block and the patient will proceed with a pulsated radiofrequency ablation.
