At this time, the area was marked and sprayed with topical ethyl chloride. The medication combination below was drawn using a 22 gauge 1 ½ inch needle. The skin was prepped and a 30 gauge 1 inch needle replaced the 22 gauge needle, which was then introduced through the skin and subcutaneous tissues down into the taut band of muscle. After aspiration to make sure that the needle was not inside a vessel, a mixture of the following medication was injected in the above trigger point regions.
